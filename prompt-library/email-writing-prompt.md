# Prompt: Professional Email Writer

## Category
Email Writing / Business Communication

## Use Case
Writing professional emails for job applications, internship requests, 
and workplace communication.

---

## Prompt

**Role:** You are a professional email writing assistant.

**Task:** Write a formal email based on the details provided below.

**Input format:**
- Sender: [Your name and background]
- Recipient: [Who you are writing to]
- Purpose: [What the email is about]
- Key points to include: [Main things to mention]
- Tone: [Formal / Semi-formal]

**Output format:**
- Subject line
- Greeting
- Body (3 short paragraphs)
- Closing

---

## Example

**Input:**
- Sender: Purbi, fresh graduate with AI certification from FutureSkills Prime
- Recipient: HR Manager at an AI startup
- Purpose: Apply for an AI internship
- Key points: My GenAI certification, interest in prompt engineering, 
  willingness to learn
- Tone: Formal

**Output:**

Subject: Application for AI Internship – Prompt Engineering Enthusiast

Dear Hiring Manager,

I am a recent graduate with a certification in Generative AI from 
FutureSkills Prime (NASSCOM) and a Microsoft AI Skill Fest badge. 
I am eager to apply for an internship opportunity at your organization.

My focus is on prompt engineering and AI content roles. I have been 
actively building my skills through platforms like deeplearning.ai, 
learnprompting.org, and Anthropic's documentation.

I would love the opportunity to contribute and grow with your team. 
Please find my details below. I look forward to hearing from you.

Warm regards,  
Purbi Biswal  
[LinkedIn] | [GitHub]

---

## Why this prompt works
- Gives the AI a clear role (email assistant)
- Specifies exact input fields so output is consistent
- Defines output structure (subject, greeting, body, closing)
- Includes a real example so anyone can understand and reuse it
